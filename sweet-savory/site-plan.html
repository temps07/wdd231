Quick Recipes for Busy Lives - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Sweet & Savory: Quick Recipes for Busy Lives

Reason:
The name reflects the dual focus of the site (sweet desserts and savory meals) while emphasizing quick, student-friendly recipes. It's memorable, alliterative, and clearly communicates the content's variety and audience (busy students/professionals).

2. Site Purpose

Primary Goal:
To provide easy, affordable, and time-saving recipes tailored for students and busy individuals.

Services/Content Offered:

  • Recipe Database: Filterable by meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert).
  • Meal-Prep Guides: Tips for batch cooking and grocery budgeting.
    • Skill-Building Resources: Cooking basics, substitution charts, and kitchen hacks.
  • Community Features: User-submitted recipes and ratings (future expansion).

3. Scenarios

Target Audience Questions:

  1. "What are some healthy breakfasts I can make in under 10 minutes before class?"
    - Solution: Quick-filter recipes by "breakfast" and "under 10 mins" tags.
  2. "How can I cook meals for the week with only $50?"
    - Solution: Budget-friendly meal plans and cost-saving tips section.

    - Solution: "Beginner-Friendly" recipe filter and video tutorials.

4. Color Schema

Color Hex Code Usage Example Swatch
Coral #FF6F61 Headings (h1, h2), buttons, accents
Soft Peach #FFF8F0 Background color, card highlights
Dark Gray #4A4A4A Body text, footer background
Gold Accent #FFD166 Hover effects, decorative elements

Why These Colors?
- Coral: Stimulates appetite (food psychology) and creates visual hierarchy.
- Peach: Warm and inviting, reduces eye strain for text-heavy content.

5. Typography

Font Family Category Usage Example
Playfair Display Serif Main headings (h1, h2) "Quick Breakfast Ideas"
Open Sans Sans-serif Body text, navigation, buttons Recipe instructions, paragraphs
Pacifico Handwritten Special headings (callouts) "Weekly Featured Recipe!"

Rationale:
- Playfair Display: Elegant and readable for titles.
- Open Sans: Clean and modern for readability on all devices.
- Pacifico: Adds playful contrast for highlights.
