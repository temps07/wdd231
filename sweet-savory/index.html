Our Mission

Sweet & Savory is dedicated to providing easy, affordable, and delicious recipes for students and busy individuals. We believe that cooking should be fun, accessible, and stress-free!

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Temple Ohanu, Sweet & Savory started as a passion project to help students eat well on a budget. Today, it has grown into a community of food lovers sharing their favorite recipes.
